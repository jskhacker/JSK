CipherX

AES-256 Encrypted File & Message Vault

PythonAES-256-GCMArgon2CryptographyCLI
🔐ABOUT CIPHERX

CipherX is a terminal-based encrypted file and message vault built for privacy-conscious users, security researchers, and anyone who needs to store sensitive data with military-grade encryption — without relying on any cloud service.

At its core, CipherX uses AES-256-GCM — the same encryption standard used by governments and financial institutions worldwide — combined with Argon2 key derivation to protect your master password against brute-force attacks.

Everything stays local. No internet connection, no servers, no third-party access. Your encrypted vault exists only on your machine, fully under your control.

⚡KEY FEATURES
  • ▸ AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption — data integrity and confidentiality guaranteed.
  • ▸ Argon2id key derivation — protects your master password against GPU brute-force attacks.
  • ▸ Encrypt and decrypt individual files or entire directories in one command.
  • ▸ Secure message storage — store sensitive notes, credentials, and secrets in encrypted vaults.
  • ▸ Zero cloud dependency — 100% offline, no data ever leaves your machine.
  • ▸ Random salt and IV generated per encryption — no two ciphertexts are alike.
  • ▸ Simple CLI — encrypt, decrypt, list vault contents with intuitive commands.
  • ▸ Secure deletion of plaintext originals after encryption.
